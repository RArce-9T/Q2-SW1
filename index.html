My Favorite Cars Racing Characters


Car Name Information Trophies won
Lightning Mcqueen Lightning Mcqueen, a rookie sensation from California, hailing from Rusteze, a brand for rusty cars, racing lots of Piston Cup tournaments, earning tons of piston cup trophies. Calling himself "Speed." 7 Piston Cups
Chick Hicks Chick Hicks, a racer hailing from htB or the Hostile Takeover Bank, a crooked racer, doing whatever he takes to take victory on the track. 1 Piston Cup (cheated)
Strip Weathers A veteran sensational racer from Dinoco, a popular company. He is known for being wealthy, fortunate, and great at racing. He is called, the "Dinoco King." 7 Piston Cups
Jackson Storm A new generation racer, hailing from IGNTR, a liquid energy drink for cars. He is very fast, and nearly beat Mcqueen, but lost to Cruz Ramirez, the successor of the late Doc Hudson and Mcqueen. 2 Piston Cups
Doc Hudson Another veteran sensational racer, hailing from no other brand but himself, marked and named as the "Hudson Hornet". He is known to be Mcqueen's late mentor and teacher, when Lightning Mcqueen got stranded in Route 66 Radiator Springs, which was handled by Doc Hudson after he retired in a car crash in his last race. 3 Piston Cups
